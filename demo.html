1. Governing Terms & Effective Date
§ 1.1 — Governing Terms
By executing this Order, the parties agree that this Order and the parties' performance hereunder shall be governed by the terms and conditions of the Agreement (contract number 0101010), which are incorporated by this reference as if fully set forth herein.
✓ 수용 가능
§ 1.2 — Effective Date
The anticipated study period is from effective date (After IRB approval) to 27 May 2030. This Agreement shall remain in full force and effect until completion of the Study or earlier termination.
✓ 수용 가능
§ 1.3 — Korean Law
The Study will be conducted by the Site in accordance with Pharmaceutical Affairs Law, Korean Good Clinical Practice Guideline, Clinical Trial Administration Guidelines, and all applicable Korean regulatory requirements, whichever affords the greater protection to the Subject.
✓ 수용 가능
2. Study Conduct
§ 2.1 — Protocol
The Protocol for the Study is Company Protocol No. 203040 entitled "A Randomized, Double-Blind, Phase 3 Study of Palcancide Versus Standard of Care in Patients with Advanced Prostate Cancer", as may be amended. When Company offers meals and beverages during domestic Investigator Meetings, the total value shall not exceed KRW 50,000 per person.
✓ 수용 가능
§ 2.3 — Use of Electronic Data Capture
Site agrees that it will enter such Study data into EDC within 5 business days 10 business days of the Subject visit1, and resolve all queries issued in the EDC system within 5 business days of the query being issued. In addition, Principal Investigator or sub-investigator shall apply electronic signature within 90 calendar days of Subject visit.
⚠ 승인 필요
§ 2.2 — Data Protection
Subject to applicable law, Company may collect and process information regarding investigators or other personnel involved in Studies. Company will notify such personnel as required by applicable law.
✓ 수용 가능
3. Reagents & Study Drug
§ 3.1 — Study Drug
Company shall provide Study Drug(s) PACD012 to Site as required by Protocol. The Site agrees and warrants that it will not seek payment or reimbursement from any Subject or third party for the cost of the Study Drug(s).
✓ 수용 가능
§ 3.2 — Required Materials
If non-Company drugs, materials or reagents are required by the Protocol ("Required Materials"), Company will reimburse Site for the Required Materials provide Required Materials free of charge and additionally compensate Site at a rate of 15% above purchase cost. Such reimbursement costs shall not exceed the amount set forth in Schedule A.
✕ 수용 불가
4. Performance Period & Enrollment
§ 4.1 — Study Commencement
The Study will commence upon execution of this Agreement, approval of the IRB/IEC, and any required approvals of applicable governmental authorities. The Site shall enroll the first Subject within 30 days of Study commencement. The Site shall use commercially reasonable efforts to enroll the first Subject within 60 days of Study commencement.
⚠ 승인 필요
5. Required Equipment
§ 5.1 — Required Equipment
The parties acknowledge that Site will require the following equipment for the performance of the Study ("Required Equipment"): TABLETS, THERMOMETERS. Such Required Equipment will be lent by Company or its representative to Site for use in the Study.
✓ 수용 가능
§ 5.2 — Delivery
As necessary, Company or its representative shall arrange for the delivery of the Required Equipment, which such equipment shall be delivered to the Site at the address provided by Site during the Site evaluation visit.
✓ 수용 가능
§ 5.3 — Installation of Required Equipment
Company or its representative shall provide for installation of the following equipment: TABLETS, THERMOMETERS.
✓ 수용 가능
§ 5.4 — Technical Support
Company or its representative shall provide for technical support and maintenance of the following equipment: TABLETS, THERMOMETERS.
✓ 수용 가능
6. Compensation

Compensation and payment terms are as set forth in Schedule A, attached hereto and incorporated herein.
✓ 수용 가능

Payments from Company to Site shall be made payable to: Ajou University Hospital (아주대학교의료원). Bank: Kookmin Bank, Account: 287937-00-003703. Site may request in writing a change in Payee information by providing Company with a signed letter from a duly authorized representative.
